Services

We build your website, host it, and keep it running. The model is simple: one plan, one point of contact, and predictable support so you don’t have to manage vendors or technical details.

Website Build (Setup)

A clean, professional website designed for clarity and credibility. Setup is a one-time project and includes launch support. Ongoing care is handled through the monthly plan.

  • New builds, redesigns, and rebuilds
  • Mobile-first layout and accessibility baseline
  • Simple navigation and clear calls-to-action
  • Launch verification (links, forms, basic performance)

Managed Hosting & Website Care

Hosting plus maintenance, monitoring, updates, and support. The goal is to keep your site stable and recoverable without ongoing effort.

  • Managed hosting + SSL/HTTPS
  • Automated backups with a restore process
  • Routine updates under a consistent policy
  • Uptime monitoring and basic health checks

Content Updates & Improvements

Small changes are included depending on your plan. For larger work, we scope and batch changes to keep cost and risk predictable.

  • Text and image updates
  • Minor layout changes
  • New pages within plan limits
  • Basic analytics or form updates (when applicable)

Optional Add-ons

Add-ons are only used when needed. They keep the core plan simple while supporting common business requests.

  • E-commerce support (scoped and standardized)
  • Email/domain setup (DNS + mailbox configuration)
  • Extra website under the same agreement
  • Out-of-scope work billed hourly
